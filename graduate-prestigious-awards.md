# Elite Graduate & Doctoral Fellowships

## 1. Stanford Knight-Hennessy Scholars Program

* **Overview:** The premiere graduate fellowship program at Stanford University, modeled after the Rhodes Scholarship.
* **Coverage:** **100% Full Tuition, Living Stipend, Academic Expenses, and Travel Allowance** for any graduate degree at Stanford (ICME MS, PhD in CS/Applied Math).
* **Key Selection Criteria:** Independence of thought, purposeful leadership, civic mindset.
* **Timeline:** Application deadline is early **October** of the year before graduate enrollment.

---

## 2. Hertz Foundation Graduate Fellowship

* **Overview:** The most prestigious doctoral fellowship in the applied physical and biological sciences, mathematics, and engineering.
* **Coverage:** Up to **$250,000 in direct total funding** over 5 years ($40,000/yr personal stipend + full tuition).
* **Key Advantage:** Complete freedom to pursue bold, high-risk research (perfect for Topological Causal Synthesism) without advisor constraint.

---

## 3. Paul & Daisy Soros Fellowships for New Americans

* **Overview:** Supports immigrants and children of immigrants pursuing graduate education in the United States.
* **Coverage:** Up to **$90,000 in funding** over two years ($25,000/yr stipend + 50% tuition covered up to $20,000/yr).

---

## 4. NSF Graduate Research Fellowship Program (NSF GRFP)

* **Overview:** Flagship national fellowship supporting outstanding graduate students in STEM.
* **Coverage:** **$159,000 total award** ($37,000/yr stipend + $16,000 cost-of-education allowance to the institution for 3 years).
* **Eligibility:** Undergraduates in senior year or 1st-year graduate students.

---

## 5. Rhodes Scholarship & Marshall Scholarship

* **Rhodes Scholarship:** Full funding for 2-3 years of postgraduate study at the **University of Oxford** (ideal for DPhil at the Oxford Centre for Topological Data Analysis).
* **Marshall Scholarship:** Full funding for 2 years of study at any university in the United Kingdom.
