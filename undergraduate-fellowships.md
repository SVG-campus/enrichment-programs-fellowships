# Undergraduate & Campus Enrichment Programs

## 1. Syracuse University Renée Crown University Honors Program

* **Overview:** The premiere university-wide academic honors program at Syracuse University.
* **Benefits:**
  - Priority class registration (select all courses first).
  - Dedicated honors housing and honors advising.
  - Access to Crown Honors Research Grants ($5,000+ for independent research).
  - Senior Honors Capstone Thesis (ideal vehicle for publishing the MERA-KMPA persistent homology research).
* **Application Strategy:** Submit an Internal Transfer/Readmission Honors Application upon re-enrollment at Syracuse with your 3.8+ GPA record.

---

## 2. Syracuse Blackstone LaunchPad & Techstars Accelerator

* **Overview:** Campus startup incubator housed in Bird Library, affiliated with the global Blackstone LaunchPad network.
* **Benefits:**
  - Non-dilutive grant funding ($1,000 to $25,000 via campus pitch competitions).
  - Direct mentorship from successful venture founders and alumni.
  - Legal, IP, and incorporation support for AetherQuant and Eminence.
* **Action:** Register AetherQuant as a Blackstone LaunchPad startup immediately upon re-enrollment.

---

## 3. Stanford Summer Undergraduate Research Fellowship (SURF)

* **Overview:** 8-week immersive summer research program at Stanford University for visiting undergraduates.
* **Benefits:**
  - Conduct research directly inside Stanford engineering and computer science labs.
  - Direct networking with Stanford faculty (ideal bridge to Prof. Gunnar Carlsson & Stanford AI Lab).
  - $8,000 stipend + covered housing and travel.
* **Application Window:** Opens November; Deadline: February 1.

---

## 4. MIT Research Experiences for Undergraduates (REU / Summer Research Program)

* **Overview:** Summer research program hosted by MIT's Department of Mathematics and CSAIL (Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory).
* **Benefits:**
  - Work on advanced topological data analysis, quantum computing, or machine learning theory.
  - Full stipend and campus housing at MIT in Cambridge, MA.

---

## 5. Barry Goldwater Scholarship in STEM Research

* **Overview:** The most prestigious national undergraduate award for students in mathematics, natural sciences, and engineering.
* **Award:** Up to $7,500 per year toward tuition and educational expenses.
* **Key Requirement:** Must demonstrate strong research career intent (Ph.D. goal) and active research output. Your papers on Topological-Quantum-Cognitive Causal Synthesism will serve as the core application proposal.
