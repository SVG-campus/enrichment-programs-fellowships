# Undergraduate & Campus Enrichment Programs (Adult & Re-Entry Friendly)

## 1. Osher Reentry Scholarship Program (Age 25+)

* **Overview:** National scholarship program specifically created for non-traditional students (aged 25+) who are returning to higher education to complete their first Bachelor's degree.
* **Award:** **$5,000 to $10,000 per year** towards tuition and fees.
* **Eligibility:** Designed specifically for adult learners returning after a gap in education. Available at Syracuse University and Columbia GS.

---

## 2. Syracuse University Renée Crown University Honors Program

* **Overview:** The premiere university-wide academic honors program at Syracuse University. Open to students of all ages.
* **Benefits:**
  - Priority class registration (select all courses first).
  - Dedicated honors advising.
  - Access to Crown Honors Research Grants ($5,000+ for independent research).
  - Senior Honors Capstone Thesis (ideal vehicle for publishing the MERA-KMPA persistent homology research).

---

## 3. Syracuse Blackstone LaunchPad & Techstars Accelerator

* **Overview:** Campus startup incubator housed in Bird Library, open to all enrolled Syracuse students regardless of age.
* **Benefits:**
  - Non-dilutive grant funding ($1,000 to $25,000 via campus pitch competitions).
  - Mentorship from successful venture founders and alumni.
  - Legal, IP, and incorporation support for AetherQuant and Eminence.

---

## 4. Columbia GS Program for Academic Leadership and Service (PALS)

* **Overview:** Full and partial tuition scholarship program designed for high-achieving non-traditional and adult students (25+) admitted to Columbia School of General Studies.
* **Benefits:** Covers tuition gaps and provides leadership mentoring within Columbia University.

---

## 5. Barry Goldwater Scholarship in STEM Research

* **Overview:** National STEM research award for undergraduate students preparing for Ph.D. careers.
* **Award:** Up to $7,500 per year. No age limit for matriculated undergraduates.
